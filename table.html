Plants Recommended Or Not
Roses Recommended since roses are very easy to care for, some effort is required.
Corpse Flower Not recommended. The corpse flower has a foul odor and the conditions it needs to bloom is unique and hard to replicate.
Venus FlyTrap Recommended, the Venus Flytrap is easy to care for since it hunts its own food. As long as you water regularly and have access to good light you should be good to go.
