Top 5 Most Beautiful Flowers In the World

Number 1: Roses

It is to no surprise that the number one spot would go to the flower of love. Its beautiful leafs of any color just capture ones soul

Number 2: Cherry Blossoms

The Cherry Blossom and its beautiful pink roses takes the number 2 spot on our list. It is no wonder it is Japan's national flower

Number 3: Azalea

Azaleas usually cluster together so in bloom they look full and vibrant and can range from yellow to pink in terms of color. It is no surprise that the Azalea flower would appear on this list.

Number 4:Bird of Paradise

The plants vibrant orange and blue petals give the flower the impression of a bird, almost like a humming birds body. It is native to certain parts of South Africa

Number 5: Bleeding Heart

This flower manages to capture anyone's attention with its heart shape and pink and white colors. This flower blooms in moist and cool climates such as the woody areas around the Appalachian Mountains
